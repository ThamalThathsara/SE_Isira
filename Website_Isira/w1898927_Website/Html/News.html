Food costs have risen dramatically in Sri Lanka

Annual food inflation in Sri Lanka increased to an all-time high of 80.1% in June this year. This becomes a major issue for people in Sri Lanka since they are facing a massive economic crisis and reports say food shortage is incoming. The government advises its citizens to prepare for a food shortage and to grow their own food. However, people in the country are suffering and it's expected to be worse in the upcoming months.

The Sri Lankan tea industry is at a big risk

The current economic crisis affects the tea industry in many ways. Workers are facing serious issues due to fuel shortage in the country and tea garden owners say they don't have enough fertilizer for their farms. This is affecting the country's economy because Sri Lanka is a major tea importer in the world. Reports say the country is losing millions of dollars due to the fall of the tea industry. Farmers say the government doesn't have a plan to secure the tea industry.
